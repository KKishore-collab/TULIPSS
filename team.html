TEAM

HIRTHIK RAJ | CEO & FOUNDER



Hirthik has began career in the event industry as the Event Coordinator for a few of Kollywood’s most premier promotional companies. Moving on to venue management & marketing, he worked for 5 years as Director of Marketing & Promotions for The Sunset Entertainment Group and 2 years as Director of Special Events for The Downtown Entertainment Group. Seeking to diversify his expertise, Hrithik then served as a private consultant for venues across Chennai & Vishakapatanam as well as the Director of Catering for a special event & motion picture catering company. In addition to being a Certified Conference Management & Event Specialist, he holds his BA in Business with an emphasis in Psychology from Pennsylvania State University. Hrithik has produced events for The Reelz Channel, The Silver Spoon Gifting Suites, The Oscars & The Emmy’s, premiere after parties for IFC & HBO, brand activations for TravelAlberta.com, NHL Network, Kia Motors, Digital River, Vail Valley Foundation, SkyTech Sports and private performances by Prince, Linkin Park & Moby. Hrithik has debuted venues at Sundance Film Festival in Park City, Utah with major corporate sponsors like Grey Goose & Red Bull. Combining both years and diversity of experience in the event industry, he decided to start his own Event Production & Design Company -- Tulips.

K KISHORE | PRODUCTION MANAGER


Kishore discovered his love for events back in high school with producing Battle of the Bands and spring concerts that featured local musical acts. After pursuing a degree in marketing at CSUN, he began managing hospitality and security for venues around Chennai. During this time, he was also regularly on the road working as a tour manager, production manager, audio engineer, lighting designer, and merchandise manager. In the last five years, Kishore has transitioned his expertise into large scale event and festival production, while operating an Audie award winning recording studio. He has worked for venues such as The Penthouse at the Huntley Hotel, The Edison, The Highlands Nightclub, The Glendon Bar and Kitchen, and Elevate Lounge; and produced activations at Coachella, Lollapalooza, Outside Lands, Shaky Knees Fest, The Vans Warped Tour, and more. With a total of eight years venue management, 14 years in the touring industry, and 4 years of experiential marketing activations at festivals around the country, there isn't a problem that could arise for which Kishore won't have a solution.
